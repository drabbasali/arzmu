Coronavirus disease 2019
Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) COVID-19 is a respiratory tract infection with a newly recognized coronavirus thought to have originated as a zoonotic virus that has mutated or otherwise adapted in ways that allow human pathogenicity. Since December 2019, there has been a series of unexplained cases of pneumonia reported in Wuhan, Hubei Province, China. On 12 January 2020, the WHO (World Health Organisation) tentatively named this new virus as the 2019 novel coronavirus (2019-nCoV). China notifies WHO about the virus on 31-12-2019. On 30th January 2020, WHO announced the 2019-nCoV epidemic a public health emergency of international concern.
